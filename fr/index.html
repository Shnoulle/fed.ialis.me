Bienvenue sur le Fediverse

un réseau social libre, décentralisé et fédéré


The fediverse is a decentralized, federated, social micro-blogging network, similar to Twitter.

It is made of a multitude of independent instances, running on different software such as Mastodon or GNU Social.

This website is a work of progress and not ready for prime time. Most link don’t work yet.
